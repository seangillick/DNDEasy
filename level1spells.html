Level 1 Spells

1st Level (Choose 6) Casting Time Range Duration Effect
Alarm
 1 minute 30 feet 8 hours Set an alarm against unwanted intrusion.
Burning Hands
 1 action Self (15-foot cone) Instantaneous A thin sheet of flames shoots forth from your outstretched fingertips.
Charm Person
 1 action 30 feet 1 hour Attempt to charm a humanoid you can see within range.
Color Spray
 1 action Self (15-foot cone) 1 round A dazzling array of flashing, colored light springs from your hand.
Comprehend Languages
 1 action Self 1 hour For the duration, you understand the literal meaning of any spoken language that you hear.
Detect Magic
 1 action Self Concentration, up to 10 minutes For the duration, you sense the presence of magic within 30 feet of you.
Disguise Self
 1 action Self 1 hour You make yourself--including your clothing, armor, weapons, and other belongings on your person--look different.
Expeditious Retreat
 1 bonus action Self Concentration, up to 10 minutes Allows you to move at an incredible pace.
False Life
 1 action Self 1 hour Bolstering yourself with a necromantic facsimile of life, you gain 1d4 + 4 temporary hit points for the duration.
Feather Fall
 1 action 60 feet 1 minute Choose up to five falling creatures within range to slow their rate of descent.
Find Familiar
 1 hour 10 feet Instantaneous You gain the service of a familiar, a spirit that takes an animal form you choose.
Floating Disk
 1 action 30 feet 1 hour Creates a circular, horizontal plane of force, 3 feet in diameter and 1 inch thick, that floats 3 feet above the ground in an unoccupied space of your choice that you can see within range.
Fog Cloud
 1 action 120 feet Concentration, up to 1 hour Create a 20-foot-radius sphere of fog centered on a point within range.
Grease
 1 action 60 feet 1 minute Slick grease covers the ground in a 10-foot square centered on a point within range and turns it into difficult terrain for the duration.
Hideous Laughter
 1 action 30 feet Concentration, up to 1 minute A creature of your choice that you can see within range perceives everything as hilariously funny and falls into fits of laughter if this spell affects it.
Identify
 1 minute Touch Instantaneous Choose one object that you must touch throughout the casting of the spell, and learn how to use them.
Illusory Script
 1 minute Touch 10 days Write on parchment, paper, or some other suitable writing material and imbue it with a potent illusion that lasts for the duration.
Jump
 1 action Touch 1 minute Touch a creature. The creature's jump distance is tripled until the spell ends.
Longstrider
 1 action Touch 1 hour Touch a creature. The target's speed increases by 10 feet until the spell ends.
Mage Armor
 1 action Touch 8 hours Touch a willing creature who isn't wearing armor, and a protective magical force surrounds it until the spell ends.
Magic Missile
 1 action 120 feet Instantaneous Create three glowing darts of magical force.
Protection from Evil and Good
 1 action Touch Concentration up to 10 minutes Until the spell ends, one willing creature you touch is protected against certain types of creatures: aberrations, celestials, elementals, fey, fiends, and undead.
Shield
 1 reaction Self 1 round An invisible barrier of magical force appears and protects you.
Silent Image
 1 action 60 feet Concentration, up to 10 minutes Create the image of an object, a creature, or some other visible phenomenon that is no larger than a 15-foot cube.
Sleep
1 action 90 feet 1 minute Sends creatures into a magical slumber.
Thunderwave
 1 action Self (15-foot cube) Instantaneous A wave of thunderous force sweeps out from you.
Unseen Servant
 1 action 60 feet 1 hour Creates an invisible, mindless, shapeless force that performs simple tasks at your command until the spell ends.
