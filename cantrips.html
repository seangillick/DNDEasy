Cantrips

Cantrips (0 level, Choose 3) Casting Time Range Duration Effect
Acid Splash
 1 action 60 feet Instantaneous Hurl a bubble of acid.
Chill Touch
 1 action 120 feet 1 round Spell attack with a ghostly, skeletal hand.
Dancing Lights
 1 action 120 feet Concentration, up to 1 minute Create torch-sized lights within range that hover.
Fire Bolt
 1 action 120 feet Instantaneous Hurl a mote of fire at a creature or object within range.
Light
 1 action Touch 1 hour Touch one object which sheds bright light in a 20-foot radius.
Mage Hand
 1 action 30 feet 1 minute A spectral, floating hand appears at a point you choose within range. Can control the hand.
Mending
 1 minute Touch Instantaneous Repairs a single break or tear in an object you touch.
Message
1 action 120 feet 1 round Point your finger toward a creature within range and whisper a message.
Minor Illusion
1 action 30 feet 1 minute Create a sound or an image of an object within range that lasts for the duration.
Poison Spray
1 action 10 feet Instantaneous Extend your hand toward a creature you can see within range and project a puff of noxious gas from your palm.
Prestidigitation
1 action 10 feet Up to 1 hour Minor magical trick that novice spellcasters use for practice.
Ray of Frost
1 action 60 feet Instantaneous A frigid beam of blue-white light streaks toward a creature within range.
Shocking Grasp
1 action Touch Instantaneous Lightning springs from your hand to deliver a shock to a creature you try to touch.
True Strike
 1 action 30 feet Concentration, up to 1 round Point a finger at a target in range for brief insight into the target's defenses.
