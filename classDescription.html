Wizards are magic-users defined and united by their spells. Their spells conjure elements from other planes of existence. Some wizards aspire to be like gods, shaping reality itself. Wizards live and die by their spells, everything else is secondary to them. They learn new spells as they experiment and grown in experience, but can also learn from other wizards, ancient tomes or inscriptions, or even ancient creatures.
