Level Features

6 When a creature within 10 meters takes damage, your ward can absorb the damage
10 When casting an abjuration apell that requires an ability check, you add your proficiency bonus to that check
14 You have advantage on saving throws against spells

The School of Abjuration emphasizes magic that blocks, banishes, or protects. Students of this school believe that tradition is about denial rather than assertion. You understand that protecting the weak and banishing evil is a proud and respected vocation.
